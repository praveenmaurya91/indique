India! The most populous democracy in the World! Think of India and you immediately think “Diversity”. India is made up of several States and Union Territories – each with its own unique language and culture. As diverse as its culture and traditions, India is also well known for its FOOD!


Indian cuisine is largely influenced by religious beliefs and regional availability of ingredients. Having seen settlers from various parts of the world over the centuries, the Indian Cuisine has also been influenced by those who came to her shores. Significant amongst those are the “Mughlai Cuisine”- a gift of the Persians & Arabs, the popular Vindaloo that came from the Portuguese, and the British – who anglicized the Indian cuisine and thanks to who we now have the Chicken Tikka Masala on menus at most Indian Restaurants!


The Redlands - located in the Coastal Region of South East Queensland, the Redlands was home to a large Aboriginal population before the arrival of the Europeans. It later became home to early settlers from England and Scotland and today it is home to many more diverse ethnicities - as diverse as India in its population!

Our menu is engineered to feature a balanced mix of classic Indian dishes and innovative contemporary creations based on fresh seasonal ingredients, prepared and cooked with traditional recipes.


We prepare authentic spice blends right on site, and we also have a theatrical charcoal-fired tandoor oven. Our mouthwatering tandoori meats and vegetables are marinated overnight and cooked in our clay tandoor oven, and you can either order for takeaway, or dine in at our restaurant.


Try our extensive range of vegetarian and gluten-free dishes and finish your meal with decadent, organic homemade desserts. We also feature a balanced wine list that is specially curated to suit all palates.

About Us

In 2006, indique began operating out of the swanky new lakeside precinct of Redlands. Since then, the site has been a part of Redlands’ contemporary history, acting as an iconic location attracting visitors from far and wide. People come to enjoy the modern style and casual elegance of the award winning indique.
